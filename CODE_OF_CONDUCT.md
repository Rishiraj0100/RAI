# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in RAI a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Public or private insults or attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community. Examples include GitHub issues, pull requests, project chat rooms, and social media platforms.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Project maintainers will determine the consequences for any behavior that they deem inappropriate, ranging from warnings to temporary or permanent bans from the project.
